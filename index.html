About the Author

Some towns keep secrets better than people do

Harper Callahan writes atmospheric Southern mysteries from her home on the North Carolina coast, where she splits her time between writing, sea-glass hunting, and trying to keep her rescue dog from stealing sandwiches off the porch.

She loves weather, good coffee, and the kind of story that leaves you a little uneasy about the house you're reading in.

The Lantern Bay Mysteries is her first series.

The Series

The Lantern Bay Mysteries

Six standalone murder mysteries set on a barrier island off the North Carolina coast. One woman, one town, and the slow unraveling of a secret that's older than both.

Book One

The Tide Remembers

Nine months into her new life on Heron Island, Nell Whitaker finds a body in the tidal pools behind her café. Avery Cole was twenty-nine, engaged, and the loudest voice against a resort development threatening the island's protected marsh. Everyone has a theory. Most of them are wrong.

Nell was an investigative journalist in another life — before a story in Washington got a source killed and she ran south to a building on the corner of Front and Pelican that she turned into the Compass Rose. She was trying to disappear. Lantern Bay had other plans.
